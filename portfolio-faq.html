Design Dialogue: Your Questions Answered

Navigate through our comprehensive guide addressing the most common inquiries about our design philosophy, process, and what to expect when creating your ideal living space.

What is the typical timeline for a complete interior design project?

+

A complete interior design project typically takes 90-120 working days from the date of receiving 60% advance payment. The timeline includes:

  • Design phase: 15-30 days
  • Material selection: 7-10 days
  • Production: 45-60 days
  • Installation: 15-20 days

Timelines may vary based on project scale, complexity, and client decision-making speed.

What is included in your interior design consultancy services?

+

Our comprehensive interior design consultancy includes:

  • Space planning and layout design
  • 2D floor plans and elevation drawings
  • 3D visualizations and walk-throughs
  • Material and color selection guidance
  • Furniture and fixture selection
  • Lighting design and planning
  • Site execution coordination
  • Vendor coordination and management
  • Quality control and supervision

What are your payment terms for interior design projects?

+

We follow a structured payment schedule:

For Woodworks:

  • 10% or ₹2,00,000 (whichever is higher) - Booking amount
  • 30% - Pre material selection
  • 30% - Final design sign-off
  • 30% - Before materials are shipped to site

For Services:

  • 100% advance payment to start service work at site

All payments are non-refundable as per our terms and conditions.

Do you work with existing furniture or only provide complete packages?

+

We offer flexible solutions based on your needs:

  • Complete Packages: Full interior design including all furniture, fixtures, and accessories
  • Partial Packages: Integration of existing furniture with new pieces
  • Consultation Only: Design guidance for spaces where you want to use existing furniture
  • Custom Solutions: Bespoke furniture designed to work with your existing pieces

During our initial consultation, we assess your existing furniture and provide recommendations on what can be incorporated and what might need replacement for a cohesive design.

How do you ensure quality control during the project?

+

We maintain stringent quality control through:

  • Regular site visits by our project managers
  • Quality checks at every stage of production
  • Using only approved vendors and materials
  • Detailed inspection before installation
  • Final quality audit before handover
  • Photographic documentation of each phase

We assign a dedicated project manager to each client who serves as a single point of contact and ensures all quality standards are met.

What happens if there are changes or revisions during the project?

+

We understand that changes may be required during the design process:

  • Design Phase: Up to 4 rounds of revisions are included in our standard package
  • During Production: Changes after work has commenced may result in additional costs and extended timelines
  • Material Changes: Any change in approved materials will be priced as per current rates
  • Structural Changes: Major changes to approved designs may require re-quotation

We recommend finalizing all design decisions before production begins to avoid additional costs and delays.

Do you provide post-installation support or warranty?

+

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-installation support:

  • Warranty Period: 1-year warranty on all woodwork and installations
  • Support: 60-day free support for any adjustments or minor fixes
  • Emergency Response: Priority support for critical issues
  • Maintenance Guidance: Detailed care instructions for all materials
  • Extended Warranty: Optional extended warranty packages available

Our warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover damages due to misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear.

How do I get started with my interior design project?

+

Starting your project is simple:

  1. Initial Consultation: Contact us via WhatsApp or phone to schedule a free consultation
  2. Site Visit: Our team visits your space to understand requirements and take measurements
  3. Design Proposal: We present initial concepts and a detailed quotation
  4. Booking: Upon approval, we proceed with the booking formalities
  5. Design Development: We create detailed designs and 3D visualizations
  6. Execution: Once designs are approved, we begin production and installation
